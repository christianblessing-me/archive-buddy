Archive Buddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Archive Buddy is a Safari extension that checks whether the webpage you are viewing has archived versions available on archive.is or related archive.today domains.

Information Archive Buddy handles

Archive Buddy does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

When you open the Archive Buddy Safari extension popup, the extension reads the URL of the active Safari tab and sends that URL to archive.is and related archive.today domains to check for archived versions.

Third-party archive services

Archive Buddy uses third-party archive services, including archive.is, archive.today, archive.ph, archive.vn, archive.fo, archive.li, and archive.md. These services may receive the webpage URL being checked, your IP address, user agent, and normal network request metadata needed to service the request.

Archive Buddy does not control those third-party services. Their own terms and privacy practices apply when requests are sent to them or when you open an archived page.

How information is used

The active page URL is used only to look up archived versions and open the archived page selected by you.

Archive Buddy does not sell your data, does not use advertising trackers, and does not use analytics services.

Storage and retention

Archive Buddy does not store your browsing history on our servers. Archive Buddy does not operate a backend server for user data.

The third-party archive services may keep their own logs or records according to their own policies.

Your choices

  • You can avoid sending a URL to archive services by not opening the Archive Buddy popup on that page.
  • You can disable Archive Buddy in Safari extension settings at any time.
  • You can uninstall Archive Buddy from your device at any time.

Children

Archive Buddy is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when Archive Buddy changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
