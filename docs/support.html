Archive Buddy Support

Help and troubleshooting for Archive Buddy.

Archive Buddy checks archive.is and related archive.today domains for archived versions of the page currently open in Safari.

Enable the Safari extension

  1. Install and open Archive Buddy once.
  2. Open Settings on your device.
  3. Go to Apps, Safari, Extensions.
  4. Select Archive Buddy and enable it.

Use Archive Buddy

  1. Open Safari and visit a webpage.
  2. Open the Safari extensions menu.
  3. Select Archive Buddy.
  4. If archived versions are found, choose one to open it in a new tab.

Troubleshooting

  • If no archive is found, archive.is may not have a snapshot for that exact URL.
  • If lookup fails, the archive service may be temporarily unavailable or rate-limiting requests.
  • If Archive Buddy does not appear in Safari, disable and re-enable the extension in Safari settings.
  • If the app was just updated, restart Safari or reinstall the app if the old extension UI still appears.

Privacy

When you open the extension popup, Archive Buddy sends the current page URL to archive.is and related archive.today domains to check for archived versions. See the Privacy Policy for details.
