Archive Buddy

Check whether the page you are viewing in Safari has archived versions available on archive.is.

How to enable

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Apps, Safari, Extensions.
  3. Enable Archive Buddy.

Privacy

When you open the extension popup, Archive Buddy sends the current page URL to archive.is and related archive.today domains to check for archived versions. The app does not store your browsing history.

Use

Open Safari, visit a page, then open Archive Buddy from the Safari extensions menu. If archived versions exist, select one to open it in a new tab.
